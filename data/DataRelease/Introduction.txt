This folder contains simulated fluxes of solar atmopsheric neutrinos accompanying the work:

"Solar Atmospheric Neutrinos and the Sensitivity Floor for Solar Dark Matter Annihilation Searches"
C.A. Arg√ºelles, G. de Wasseige, A. Fedynitch, B.J.P. Jones
https://arxiv.org/abs/1703.07798


We present a limited simulation release in two folders:
PrePropagation:
 -solar atmospheric neutrino fluxes predicted at each impact parameter for a variety of primary cosmic ray models and for each neutrino flavor.
PostPropagation 
 -solar atmopsheric neutrino fluxes after propagation through the Sun and between Sun and Earth, aggregated over impact factors.

Both folders contain a README with format information, as well as summary plots and scripts with which they were generated, in jupyter notebook format.
